Getting started:

For getting started you have to register your company and service in LSIM.

1.For registering company we need only Company name.After registeration you will get partnerid.This id represents your company
2.For registering service we need:
   service name (exp. hava proqnozu)
   service description (explain service)
   service detail (explain service in detail, exp. You will be charged daily 0.10 azn)
   feedbackurl (when user press yes or no user will redirect to feedbackurl page,like index.php)
   charge amount (service price)
   servicetype (daily,weekly,monthly etc.)
   After service registration you will get serviceid


Usage:

Request:

type:POST

url:http://apps.lsim.az/landingpage/partner.jsp?actionname=&serviceid=&partnerid=&partnertransactionid=

parameters:

actionname:
this parameter represents action name

serviceid:
this parameter represents service which is registered in LSIM and user will be redirected to.You will get this id from LSIM

partnerid:
represents company which is registered in LSIM.You will get this id from LSIM

partnertransactionid:.This is unique id which is registered in your database.



If client press yes we will send "1",partnertransactionid and msisdn to your vpnurl which is registered before in LSIM. And you will find request by this transactionid in your table and do some operations what you need.
