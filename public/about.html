Project Drift

Clouds moving, stars in the night sky, traffic, people walking in the streets captured in time-lapse mode are breathtaking.

Especially the Northern Lights. We all love it

Project Drift aims to provide a great collection of stunning time-lapse videos which are created by the community for everyone. Creators can submit their videos submission and allow a strong community to use those videos with or without credits to the owner.

We are a small team of 2 and love this platform and hope to continue this for a longterm WITHOUT annoying Ads. However, a small contribution from your side will help us to keep the servers and infrastructure running without issues.

Want to help us? you can reach out to the team for any of these:-
  • Donation for Server Cost
  • More videos for the community
  • Feedback / Suggestions / Criticism (yes, we accept this also)

As a small token to recognize your effort, your name and link to your work profile will be mentioned in the weekly newsletter of Project Drift contributors list.

Note:

Video content on the platform has been contributed by creators. We try our best to maintain the integrity of your work and respect it. If you see that content here violates the copyright of your personal work, do email us. We will work together to resolve it.
