StigmaNFT is a collection of cards on the Cardano blockchain.

The world of Stigma is inspired by the movie The Gladiator, but... what if the Roman Empire happened in a dieselpunk future?

Card set #1

HOW IT WORKS

The first deck of cards is composed of 37 unique cards, minted in different quantities. The total number of minted tokens for the deck will be 5000.
Purchasing the set costs 360ADA. Every purchase will grant you all the cards in the set, with a random chance to find a special version for some cards.

If you joined our pre-sale list you will be granted a 30% discount on the set, and a gift consisting of a special card to celebrate the project’s launch.




ABOUT THE PROJECT

In the distant future, a man-caused accident turned Earth into a poisonous land. Nature mutated to adapt and retook most of the territories. Humans survive in elevated cities inside the declining Roman Empire, while soldiers fight at the borders of humanity to conquer resources.
And that’s where our story begins...

Stigma was born from the collaboration with a talented concept artist (@cardano_png) who dedicated six months to worldbuilding and painting each illustration pictured on the cards.
