Contribute to the Project Drift
Project Drift is and will always be for creators and by creators.

We publish high-quality time-lapse videos and credit to the owner of videos is provided. Be a part of Project Drift Team by contributing videos you love and created by you.

As a small token, your name and link to your work profile will be mentioned in weekly newsletter of Project Drift contributors list.

We appreciate if you read our Video Submission Guidelines:-
  • Video submission should be your original, please do not copy someone's else hard work and submit it.
  • Your submission should be free to use by others.
  • Let us know if you want the user to provide credit to your video. We will include in the video description.
  • Our team is very small, we may take up to 1 week to review your submission. Be patient, we will get back to you surely.
  • Have questions before submission? Sure, disturb us here.
